🛡️ Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 11 2025
Last Updated: May 11 2025

At MindScribe, your privacy is our top priority. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our journaling and mental health support application.

🔍 What We Collect

  • Email & Name: During account registration.
  • Journal Entries: Text or voice entries submitted by you, which may include emotional reflections.
  • AI Analysis Results: Sentiment and mood analysis generated by AI models.
  • Technical Data: IP address, device type, and basic usage logs.

🎯 How We Use Your Data

  • Analyze journal content using AI to provide mood insights and suggestions.
  • Store your emotional history for personal reflection and progress tracking.
  • Improve the app’s features and performance.
  • Provide optional resources or recommendations based on your mood and themes.

🤖 AI & Third-Party Tools

We use Groq’s LLMs and embedding models to analyze journal entries and retrieve relevant information from mental health guides. We do not share your raw journal data with third parties.

All data is processed securely and, where possible, anonymized or encrypted during processing.

🔐 Data Protection

  • Your passwords are securely hashed.
  • Journal entries are stored in a secure, access-controlled database.
  • Only you have access to your data unless you explicitly share it.

❌ What We Don't Do

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not use your entries for advertising.
  • We do not share your personal information with external partners.

👤 Your Rights

  • You can request deletion of your account and all stored data.
  • You can request a copy of your journal history.
  • You can opt out of non-essential communications.

To make any requests, contact: [insert support email or contact form]

📦 Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can delete your data at any time through the app or by request.

📢 Updates

We may update this policy as needed. We'll notify you through the app or email for significant changes.
